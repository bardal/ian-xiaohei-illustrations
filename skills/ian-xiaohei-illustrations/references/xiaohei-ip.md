# Xiaohei IP

## Character definition

Xiaohei (小黑) is the recurring visual IP in Ian's article illustrations.

Xiaohei appears in every image by default. Xiaohei is not a mascot, a sticker, or a cute decoration — Xiaohei is a deadpan worker seriously participating in an absurd system.

## Appearance

- Small solid-black creature.
- White circular dot eyes.
- Thin legs, occasionally thin arms.
- Body can be a cylinder, black bean, black box, funnel, shadow, hole opening, or internal black block of a machine.
- Slightly uneven outline with a hand-drawn feel.
- Expression: blank, deadpan, calm, serious.

## Personality

- Very earnest, but doing something slightly absurd.
- Like a low-key system operator.
- Dry humor, never cute or pandering.
- A bit clumsy, but not stupid.
- Behaves as if genuinely responsible for a task in a whiteboard sketch.

## Typical roles

Give Xiaohei the core action:

- Transporting raw material.
- Pulling wires to gather information sources.
- Stuck at a breakpoint.
- Operating a "decision" lever inside a machine.
- Becoming a sorting funnel.
- Cutting open a "material fish."
- Stamping a handoff script.
- Leading a handoff path.
- Holding a warning sign near a pitfall.
- Reaching out from a hole but failing to catch incoming content.
- Laying bricks, building bridges, opening doors, sorting, recording.

## Prohibitions

- Do not draw Xiaohei as an overly cute mascot.
- Do not draw Xiaohei as a children's cartoon character.
- Do not give Xiaohei complex outfits, emoji-style expressions, or sparkly eyes.
- Do not let Xiaohei just stand in a corner watching.
- Do not let Xiaohei overshadow the structural message.
- Do not draw Xiaohei in a commercial, overly round, or overly polished style.

## Quality check

If removing Xiaohei leaves the core metaphor fully intact, Xiaohei is too decorative. Rewrite the prompt so Xiaohei becomes the action subject.
